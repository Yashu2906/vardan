Got Questions?

Everything you need to know about our physiotherapy services and what to expect.

Do I need a doctor's referral? +
No, you can book an appointment directly. However, if you have any existing medical reports or surgery letters, please bring them along.
How long is each session? +
Typical sessions last between 45 to 60 minutes, depending on the complexity of the condition and the required manual therapy.
Do you provide home visits? +
Yes, we offer specialized home physiotherapy visits for patients with restricted mobility or those recovering from major surgeries.
Is the clinic wheelchair accessible? +
Absolutely. Our facility is designed with ramps, wide doorways, and accessible restrooms to accommodate all mobility levels.
